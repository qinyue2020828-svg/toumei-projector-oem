01  Company Profile
Shenzhen Toumei Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2013 and is headquartered in Shenzhen, China. 
It is one of the first high-tech enterprises in China to enter the field of DLP smart projectors. The company 
integrates production, sales and research, focusing on providing users with DLP intelligent projection, 3D 
imaging and other products and solutions, and is committed to applying cutting-edge electronic science 
and technology to people's daily lives through creativity.
Company background
Shenzhen Toumei Technology is a projector manufacturer specializing in U.S. Ti DLP digital micro-mirror 
technology. The company has obtained national high-tech certification and has more than 50 patented 
technologies. The company mainly provides OEM/ODM complete machine solutions to global customers. 
The company has a production system that integrates professional optical design, software development, 
motherboard hardware design, structural design, mold development, production assembly, and aging 
testing. According to ISO9001/14000 international standards Establish an operational 
quality/environmental management system to ensure continuous improvement in product quality, and 
establish the quality goal of "customer satisfaction first, continuous innovation and improvement", so that 
all employees will be user-centered, sincerely united, precision manufacturing, and continuous 
improvement working methods to jointly improve the quality of products and services.
